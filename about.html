ABOUT US

Where Science Meets Performance

PEAQX Physiotherapy and Performance Sciences is owned and operated by AGIS Healthcare LLP, with a vision to redefine rehabilitation, performance enhancement, and preventive healthcare through evidence-based physiotherapy and innovative treatment approaches.

The name PEAQ stands for Performance Enhancement Assessment and Qynetix, reflecting our core philosophy: combining scientific assessment, advanced rehabilitation techniques, and performance-driven strategies to deliver optimal outcomes for every individual.

PEAQX was founded by Dr. A. Gayas (PT), with Dr. S. R. Raza (PT), Dr. Vibhor K. Singh (PT), and Dr. Iqbal Haider (PT) as Co-Founders. Together, our team of experts and dedicated physiotherapists shares a common mission: to provide honest, effective, and patient-centered care with the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.

At PEAQX we believe that every patient deserves personalized attention and the best possible treatment. We are driven by passion, hard work, and a deep commitment to helping people overcome pain, restore function, and achieve their physical potential. Our goal is not just to treat symptoms, but to address the root cause of problems and empower individuals to live healthier and more active lives.

We are committed to never disappointing our patients. With advanced physiotherapy techniques, modern equipment, and a holistic approach, we strive to deliver measurable and lasting results. We firmly believe and proudly demonstrate that physiotherapy has the power to heal, rehabilitate, and transform lives.

PEAQX Physiotherapy and Performance Sciences - where science meets performance, clinical excellence meets luxury care and recovery meets refined experience.

Personalized Treatment Design

Customized, progressive programs built on clinical findings, lifestyle demands, and recovery goals.
