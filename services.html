Our Treatment Services

Each service is tailored to address your specific condition, goals, and lifestyle to ensure optimal results.

Back Adjustment

Expert spinal manipulation techniques to restore proper alignment, relieve tension, and improve overall spinal health. Our therapists use precise, controlled movements to address misalignments that cause pain and restricted mobility.

  • Reduces back and neck pain
  • Improves posture and alignment
  • Enhances nervous system function

Corrective Exercise

Personalized exercise programs designed to address muscular imbalances, improve movement patterns, and prevent future injuries. We focus on strengthening weak areas and improving flexibility where needed.

  • Corrects muscular imbalances
  • Prevents recurring injuries
  • Enhances athletic performance

Muscle Injury Treatment

Comprehensive treatment for strains, tears, and muscle-related conditions to accelerate healing. We combine manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and modalities to optimize tissue repair and restore function.

  • Accelerates tissue healing
  • Reduces inflammation and pain
  • Restores full range of motion

Acupuncture

Traditional Chinese medicine technique using fine needles to stimulate specific points on the body, reduce pain, and promote natural healing processes. Effective for both acute and chronic conditions.

  • Natural pain relief
  • Reduces stress and tension
  • Promotes overall wellness

Bone Injury Rehabilitation

Specialized rehabilitation for fractures and bone-related injuries to restore full function. We work closely with orthopedic specialists to ensure proper healing and progressive strengthening throughout recovery.

  • Safe, progressive recovery
  • Prevents complications
  • Restores strength and mobility

Joint Mobilization

Gentle manual therapy techniques to improve joint mobility, reduce stiffness, and restore normal joint mechanics. Particularly effective for arthritis, post-surgical stiffness, and chronic joint problems.

  • Improves joint flexibility
  • Reduces pain and stiffness
  • Enhances functional mobility

Postural Correction

Address poor posture habits to prevent pain and improve overall body alignment. We analyze your posture, identify problem areas, and create a customized plan to retrain your body for optimal alignment.

  • Prevents chronic pain
  • Improves breathing and circulation
  • Enhances appearance and confidence

FAQ

What You May Want To Know

Common questions about our services, treatments, and what to expect during your visit.

Your first visit will include a comprehensive assessment where we evaluate your condition, medical history, and goals. We'll perform physical tests, discuss your symptoms, and create a personalized treatment plan. The session typically lasts 60-90 minutes and may include some initial treatment.

In most cases, you don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist. However, some insurance plans may require one for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider. If you have specific medical conditions or recent surgery, a referral can be helpful.

The number of sessions varies based on your condition, severity, and individual response to treatment. Acute injuries might require 4-6 sessions, while chronic conditions may need 8-12 or more. We'll discuss your expected treatment timeline during your initial assessment and adjust as needed.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We'll verify your coverage before your first appointment and help you understand your benefits. For plans we don't directly accept, we can provide detailed receipts for you to submit for reimbursement.

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For lower body treatments, shorts are ideal. For upper body or spine work, a tank top or t-shirt works well. Avoid tight jeans, restrictive clothing, or clothing with lots of buttons or zippers.

While some techniques may cause temporary discomfort, treatment should not be painful. You may feel some pressure or mild discomfort during manual therapy, which is normal. We always work within your comfort level and adjust our approach as needed. Many patients report feeling relief during or immediately after treatment.

We use a combination of manual therapy, exercise therapy, education, and pain management techniques to help reduce pain, improve function, and enhance quality of life.
